###### Chemical Reactor

-   -   Chemical Reactor** used to combine two
        `![](/mods/techreborn/lava_cell.png)`{=mediawiki}
        **[Fluids](items:fluid "wikilink")** into a third. It has
        input rate of 512 FE/tick and internal buffer of 40.000 FE.
