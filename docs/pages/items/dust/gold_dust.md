###### Gold Dust {#gold_dust}

-   -   Gold Dust\*\* is a dust added by TechReborn. It is used to
        produce `<mcitem>`{=html}minecraft:gold_ingot`</mcitem>`{=html}
        and some alloys.

\\\\
