###### Titanium Dust

- Titanium Dust** is a source for
        <Mcitem id="techreborn:titanium_ingot"/>,
        needed for many of the endgame machines.

The source of the first few
<Mcitem id="techreborn:titanium_dust"/> will be
crafting from
<Mcitem id="techreborn:small_pile_of_titanium_dust"/>

Later, <Mcitem id="techreborn:titanium_dust"/> is
best aquired by crafting from
<Mcitem id="techreborn:uu_matter"/>
