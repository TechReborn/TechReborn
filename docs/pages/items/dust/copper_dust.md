###### Copper Dust {#copper_dust}

-   -   Copper Dust\*\* is a dust added by TechReborn. It is mainly used
        to produce
        `<mcitem>`{=html}techreborn:copper_ingot`</mcitem>`{=html} or
        some alloys.

\\\\
