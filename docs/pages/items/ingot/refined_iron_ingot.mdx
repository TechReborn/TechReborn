### Smelting Recipe

Main source of **Refined Iron Ingot** is smelting
<Mcitem id="minecraft:iron_ingot"/> in any kind of
Furnaces, including vanilla Furnace,
<Mcitem id="techreborn:iron_furnace"/> or
<Mcitem id="techreborn:electric_furnace"/>.
