###### Refined Iron Ingot

- Refined Iron Ingot** is an ingot used in many TR recipes. It
        could be acquired by smelting vanilla
        <Mcitem id="minecraft:iron_ingot"/>.

\\\\

### Smelting Recipe

Main source of **Refined Iron Ingot** is smelting
<Mcitem id="minecraft:iron_ingot"/> in any kind of
Furnaces, including vanilla Furnace,
<Mcitem id="techreborn:iron_furnace"/> or
<Mcitem id="techreborn:electric_furnace"/>.

`<recipe>` size 1x1 input minecraft:iron_ingot output
techreborn:refined_iron_ingot tool techreborn:iron_furnace
`</recipe>` \\\\ \\\\
