###### Tin Ingot

- Tin Ingot** is an ingot added by Tech Reborn and used in
        several crafting recipes.

Main source of **Tin Ingot** is smelting
<Mcitem id="techreborn:tin_dust"/> in any kind of

<Mcitem id="techreborn:iron_furnace"/> or
<Mcitem id="techreborn:electric_furnace"/>.
