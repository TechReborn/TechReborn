The **Diesel Cell** is a fluid cell added
by Tech Reborn. It is used to produce energy in
<Mcitem id="techreborn:diesel_generator"/> or for
further processing into
<Mcitem id="techreborn:nitrodiesel_cell"/>.

##### Recipe

<Mcitem id="techreborn:distillation_tower"/> recipe:
\\\\ 16x<Mcitem id="techreborn:oil_cell"/> +
16x<Mcitem id="techreborn:empty_cell"/> -\>
16x<Mcitem id="techreborn:diesel_cell"/> +
15x<Mcitem id="techreborn:sulfuric_acid_cell"/> +
<Mcitem id="techreborn:glyceryl_cell"/>

##### Usage

<Mcitem id="techreborn:diesel_cell"/> is used to
generate energy. 1 cell or bucket will generate 128k Energy in
<Mcitem id="techreborn:diesel_generator"/>.

\\\\

It is also used for further processing in
<Mcitem id="techreborn:chemical_reactor"/> recipe:
\\\\ <Mcitem id="techreborn:diesel_cell"/> +
<Mcitem id="techreborn:glyceryl_cell"/> -\>
5x<Mcitem id="techreborn:nitrodiesel_cell"/>
