The **Nitro Diesel Cell** is a fluid
cell added by Tech Reborn. It is used to generate energy in
<Mcitem id="techreborn:diesel_generator"/>.

##### Recipe

<Mcitem id="techreborn:chemical_reactor"/> recipe:
\\\\ <Mcitem id="techreborn:diesel_cell"/> +
<Mcitem id="techreborn:glyceryl_cell"/> -\>
5x<Mcitem id="techreborn:nitrodiesel_cell"/>

##### Usage

<Mcitem id="techreborn:nitrodiesel_cell"/> is used
to generate energy. 1 cell or bucket will generate 400k Energy in
<Mcitem id="techreborn:diesel_generator"/>.
