##### Silver Ore

- Silver Ore** is a rare ore found in the Overworld on layers
        20-60. When smelted, it produces one
        <Mcitem id="techreborn:silver_ingot"/>. When
        put in a <Mcitem id="techreborn:grinder"/>,
        it will produce 2x
        <Mcitem id="techreborn:silver_dust"/>.
